West Coast Swing

In my free time I enjoy dancing West Coast Swing (WCS). WCS is a partner dance which is danced both socially and competitively. It is known for its high level of musicality and distinctively elastic look. It is so much fun to dance and the community is just awesome. Jack & Jill are competitions where you draw random partners and dance to random songs, Strictly Swing are competitions where you pick your partner but dance to random songs.
