Getting Started

First Time Setup

  1. Open Weight Visualizer
  2. Tap "Check App Permissions" if you see "No Weight Data"
  3. Follow the steps to grant Health access
  4. Your weight history will appear automatically

No Data Showing?

  • Check that you have weight data in Apple Health
  • Verify Weight Visualizer has Health access: Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Data Access & Devices > Weight Visualizer
  • Try the demo mode to explore features while troubleshooting

Common Questions

Why can't I see my weight data?

Weight Visualizer needs permission to read weight data from Apple Health. If you previously denied access, you'll need to enable it manually in iPhone Settings.

Can I add weight data directly in the app?

No. Weight Visualizer is read-only and displays data from Apple Health. Add weight entries through the Health app or compatible smart scales.

Why does the app say "Health Access Denied"?

This appears when Health access hasn't been granted. Tap "Check App Permissions" for step-by-step instructions to fix this.

How do I change units between kg and lbs?

Tap the settings gear icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner to switch between metric and imperial units.

What's demo mode?

Demo mode shows realistic sample data so you can explore all features without connecting your Health data. Exit demo mode anytime by tapping the demo badge.

Features

Chart Interaction

  • Tap any data point to see details
  • Drag to explore different points
  • Tap outside the chart to deselect
  • Use landscape mode for full-screen charts

Time Ranges

  • Choose from 1W, 1M, 6M, 1Y, 5Y, or All time
  • Tap the calendar icon for custom date ranges

Statistics

  • Tap minimum or maximum cards to highlight those points on the chart
  • All stats update automatically when you change time ranges

Troubleshooting

App crashes or won't open

  1. Force close the app
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Reopen Weight Visualizer

Charts not updating

  1. Pull down on the main screen to refresh
  2. Check that new data exists in Apple Health
  3. Try switching time ranges and switching back

Missing recent data

Apple Health data sync can sometimes be delayed. Wait a few minutes and pull down to refresh.

System Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • Apple Health with weight data
  • iPhone (iPad not officially supported)
