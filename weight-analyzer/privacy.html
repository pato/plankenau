Overview

Weight Visualizer is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your health data should remain private and under your control. This privacy policy explains how Weight Visualizer handles your information.

Data We Access

Weight Visualizer requests read-only access to your weight data stored in Apple Health. Specifically:

  • Weight measurements and timestamps
  • This data is only read from Apple Health, never modified or deleted

Data We Don't Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No account creation or registration required
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers beyond what's required for basic app functionality

How Your Data Is Used

Your weight data is used exclusively within the app to:

  • Generate interactive charts and visualizations
  • Calculate statistics (averages, minimums, maximums, trends)
  • Provide insights about your weight patterns over time

All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Storage and Security

  • All weight data remains in Apple Health
  • Weight Visualizer does not store your health data separately
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage or backup of your health information
  • The app works completely offline after initial setup

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your weight data never leaves your device except through Apple Health's own sharing mechanisms (which you control independently).

Demo Mode

Weight Visualizer includes a demo mode with simulated weight data for testing purposes. This sample data:

  • Is generated locally on your device
  • Is not based on real user data
  • Is deleted when you exit demo mode
  • Is never transmitted or stored permanently

Third-Party Services

Weight Visualizer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Weight Visualizer does not knowingly collect or process data from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences and relies on Apple Health, which has its own age restrictions.

Your Rights and Control

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • Revoke Weight Visualizer's access to Apple Health at any time through iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Weight Visualizer
  • Delete the app to remove all local app data
  • Your weight data remains in Apple Health regardless of app installation status

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Apple Health Integration

Weight Visualizer integrates with Apple Health in accordance with Apple's HealthKit framework policies. Apple Health data sharing is governed by your iOS privacy settings and Apple's own privacy policies.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Weight Visualizer's data practices, you can contact us through the App Store or at the support channels listed in the app.

Legal Basis

Where applicable under data protection laws, our legal basis for processing your weight data is your explicit consent, which you provide when granting the app access to Apple Health.

Weight Visualizer is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your health information.
